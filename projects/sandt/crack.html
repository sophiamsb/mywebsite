CRACK UP – CRACK DOWN

Space Design

2019

On the occasion of the 33rd edition of The Ljubljana Biennial, Slavs and Tatars curated an exhibition focused on graphic editions, prints, and other works on paper. I was in charge to prepare the installation and production of artists' propositions.
-> The mockups and production of Hamja Ahsan’s installation The Aspergian Referundum related to his book Shy Radicals.
-> The video installation of the Yugoslav comedy show Top Lista Nadrealista retrospective, which originally ran from late 1984 to 1991.
