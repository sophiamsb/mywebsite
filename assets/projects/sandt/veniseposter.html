Poster for Alternative Biennales

Poster

Slavs and Tatars Internship

2019

On the occasion of the 58th Venice Biennale exhibition, the organization had request posters proposition for alternative biennales. The poster proposes an alternative time as if east european countries had participated in the 1993 Venice Biennale. Those countries were not allowed to exhibit from the past biennales during URSS period. Written in Russian, the title means “ more it changes… ” and the monument represents the logo of the eastern countries organization C.E.I (community of independent states).
